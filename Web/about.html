Hi! I'm Evan Todd.

I'm the lead developer for Lemma. I started this project in my spare time in October 2010. The goal is to release a short and sweet single-player game on Desura and Steam.

Right, on to the game then. Lemma is an immersive first-person parkour game set on a mysterious desolate island. Every parkour move executed has the potential to modify the environment; surfaces pop in and out of existence at will. As you delve deeper into the island, you communicate via text message with a mysterious person stranded somewhere on the island with you.

Who's worked on this game? The music was composed by the lovely and talented Jack Menhorn. He also helped with many of the sound effects. I pulled the other sounds from FreeSound and edited them in Audacity. The full attribution list is available with the game.
