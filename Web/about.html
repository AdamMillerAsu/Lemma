About

What is this?

Lemma is an immersive first-person parkour game. Every parkour move has the potential to modify the environment; surfaces pop in and out of existence at will.

Where can I buy it?

Hopefully, on Steam and Desura very soon. Help me make it a reality on Steam!

Who are you?

Hi! I'm Evan, an independent developer from Ohio. I started this project in my spare time in October 2010. The goal is to release a short but sweet single-player game on Steam and Desura.

How can I contact you?

  • et1337@gmail.com
  • @et1337

Who else worked on this game?

The music was composed by the lovely and talented Jack Menhorn. He also helped with many of the sound effects. I mixed the other sounds from FreeSound samples. The full attribution list is available here.

Is Lemma open source?

Mostly yes! The engine, editor, and some of the assets are open source, right here on GitHub! Most of the actual game content is not open source largely due to licensing issues.
